Professional Profile

Creating Value Through Development

A civil engineer by profession who has been a C-Level executive for more than 16 years. With over 18 years' global experience, Chris took a lead in planning, structuring (financial and legal), conceptualizing and executing mixed use developments, satellite cities and urban nodes in the Middle East and Africa.
